Artist Statement

I deliver a memory as a memory. Attempting to live in the past is inconducive; thus the idea is to allow only a holographic bridge.

I have a BA in mathematics from NYU Courant. People often ask if I am trying to incorporate math into art. I say no, because they are simultaneously separate and already the same.
